Understand how World GDP per Capita and Mortality rate has changed in the last two decades

This narrative visualization is structured as an interactive slideshow. Each scene is presented with a title at the top of the slide, a line graph demonstrating the trend, and annotations highlighting the anomalies in the trend.

Using the "previous" and "next" buttons of the scene, the viewer can navigate to the next slide or previous slide

For triggers, Each data point is marked as a solid circle, with the option to hover over the point to see more detailed data displayed on top of the data point.

The last slide provides user an opportunity to explore the GDP and Mortality Rate data for individual countries.

Sources:

  • GDP per capita, PPP (current international $)
  • Mortality rate, infant (per 1,000 live births)
